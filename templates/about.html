About Personality Prediction

Welcome to Personality Prediction, your guide to understanding and predicting personalities based on the Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) dataset.

What is MBTI?

The Myers-Briggs Type Indicator is a psychological tool that categorizes individuals into 16 personality types based on their preferences in four dichotomies: Extraversion/Introversion, Sensing/Intuition, Thinking/Feeling, and Judging/Perceiving.


How Does Our Prediction Work?

We use advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques and machine learning models to analyze text data associated with MBTI personalities. Our model predicts your personality type based on the patterns in your input text.
